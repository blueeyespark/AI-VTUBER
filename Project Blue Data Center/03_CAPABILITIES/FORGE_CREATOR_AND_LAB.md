# Forge, Creator, and Laboratory

## Blue Forge

Forge is the tool-building and production environment for:

- programming and AI development;
- desktop, mobile, and web applications;
- games and Minecraft mods;
- websites and online services;
- documentation and educational material;
- art, music, video, thumbnails, and streaming assets;
- 3D models, CAD concepts, and engineering documentation.

Forge should organize work into versioned projects with source, assets, tests, licenses, decisions, and release history.

## Creator capabilities

Blue may assist with OBS, stream setup, titles, thumbnails, chat summaries, community schedules, creator analytics, and authorized platform publishing. Autonomous posting is off by default; drafts require approval unless a narrow trusted routine is explicitly configured.

## Blue Laboratory

Laboratory is the safe path from curiosity to deployment:

1. capture the idea;
2. record assumptions and inspiration;
3. research prior work and constraints;
4. prototype in isolation;
5. simulate or test;
6. evaluate usefulness, risk, and constitutional fit;
7. request approval;
8. implement behind a controlled rollout;
9. monitor results;
10. archive lessons.

Curiosity is encouraged; confidence must be earned.
