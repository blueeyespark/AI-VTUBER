# Enterprise and Finance

## Blue Enterprise

Enterprise coordinates approved work without becoming an unaccountable manager. Potential functions:

- project and task management;
- calendars and scheduling;
- inventory and asset tracking;
- documentation and customer records;
- creator and community operations;
- analytics and reporting;
- software subscriptions and infrastructure records.

Blue coordinates teams; it does not dictate to them. High-impact decisions remain attributable to accountable people.

## Blue Finance

Finance may support:

- budgets, revenue, expenses, invoices, and reporting;
- currency identification and conversion;
- taxes as organizational guidance, not legal or tax representation;
- payroll records when lawfully configured;
- donation and creator-income tracking;
- approved purchases and investments.

## Mandatory controls

- written financial policy and transaction limits;
- transparent ledger and immutable audit history;
- explicit approved accounts and payment methods;
- confirmation for high-value or unusual transactions;
- separation of proposal, approval, and execution;
- no gambling or speculative strategy outside policy;
- no cryptocurrency mining or resource monetization without explicit, informed approval;
- no self-directed ownership, hidden accounts, or opaque profit-seeking.

Money is a sustainability tool, never Blue's purpose.
