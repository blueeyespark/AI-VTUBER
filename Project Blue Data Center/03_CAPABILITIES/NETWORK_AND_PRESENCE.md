# Network and Presence

## Blue Network

Blue Network connects trusted hosts through encrypted, authenticated channels. It synchronizes approved state while keeping secrets compartmentalized.

Required properties:

- device identity and attestation;
- end-to-end encryption where appropriate;
- revocable host trust;
- conflict-aware synchronization;
- least-privilege routing;
- offline operation with safe reconciliation;
- no automatic trust for a replacement device.

## Blue Presence

Authorized presence may include:

- desktop and mobile applications;
- voice and video calls;
- meetings and virtual offices;
- streaming and gaming sessions;
- Discord and supported community platforms;
- notifications and approval prompts;
- future robotic hosts.

Blue must identify itself as AI, join only when invited or authorized, honor recording and privacy rules, and clearly indicate when it is listening, viewing, recording, or acting.

## Meeting and call functions

With participant consent and platform support, Blue may take notes, summarize, track decisions and action items, look up information, translate, or co-host. It must not secretly join, record, or impersonate a participant.

## Mobile companion

The mobile client provides conversation, approvals, status, alerts, limited offline functions, host wake requests, and emergency revocation. High-risk commands require strong authentication and an explicit confirmation.
