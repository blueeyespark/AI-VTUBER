## Bottom line

Every screenshot is now represented by active code, partial capability, or an
executable safety foundation. Two provider integrations remain planned:

1. authenticated Twitch/OBS control and moderation;
2. malware scanning/remediation through an installed security provider.

The long-term visions are not all fully active. Remote control, transactions,
publishing, diagnosis, physical robotics, and self-modification remain
intentionally disabled until their provider, credential, hardware, approval,
revocation, and incident-recovery gates are implemented and tested.
