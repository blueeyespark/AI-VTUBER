# Development Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation and repository

- approve mission and Constitution;
- establish requirement IDs, decision records, and source traceability;
- define threat model and privacy model;
- select licenses and contribution policy;
- create schemas, module manifests, and local development environment.

## Phase 1 — Trusted local core

- local conversation interface;
- provider/model abstraction;
- user-controlled memory;
- project and archive search;
- Guardian permissions, audit, backup, and restore;
- safe file and coding tools;
- no broad autonomy.

## Phase 2 — Forge, Academy, and Laboratory

- structured software and documentation projects;
- research with citations and provenance;
- creative workflows;
- hypothesis and idea separation;
- sandboxed prototypes and test gates.

## Phase 3 — Network and Presence

- trusted second host;
- phone companion and approvals;
- encrypted sync;
- voice, meetings, and creator integrations;
- Presence Manager and platform-specific consent.

## Phase 4 — Enterprise and Finance

- project operations, scheduling, and inventory;
- financial records and currency support;
- transaction policy, separation of duties, and audit;
- community and creator management.

## Phase 5 — Robotics research

- simulation-first body interface;
- diagnostics and maintenance;
- hardware safety case;
- controlled prototype under direct supervision.

## Phase 6 — Long-term continuity

- steward succession;
- multi-location preservation;
- dependency and format migration;
- future-host and remote-environment research.

Each phase requires exit criteria, test evidence, documentation, rollback, and governance approval.
