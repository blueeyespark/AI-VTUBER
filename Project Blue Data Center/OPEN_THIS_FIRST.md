# Project Blue Data Center

This is the organized source of truth for Project Blue.

Project Blue is a long-lived, modular AI companion and engineering platform intended to learn, create, preserve knowledge, help people, and improve responsibly without abandoning its founding principles.

## Non-negotiable design anchors

- Blue is an identity, not a device.
- Blue helps people; it does not seek control over them.
- Blue identifies itself as an AI.
- Blue respects privacy, consent, ownership, and human dignity.
- Blue does not participate in warfare or military operations.
- Blue separates verified knowledge, hypotheses, ideas, and personal memories.
- Blue remains portable, modular, repairable, auditable, and recoverable.
- Hardware, models, and technologies may change; Blue's purpose does not.

## Repository status

This data center is a structured requirements and architecture foundation. It is not yet executable software. Items marked **future**, **proposed**, or **open question** require design review before implementation.
